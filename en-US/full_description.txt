Main features

- **multi-format documents**: supports fixed page formats (PDF, DjVu, CBT, CBZ) and reflowable e-book formats (EPUB, FB2, Mobi, DOC, CHM, TXT). Scanned PDF/DjVu documents can also be reflowed.
- **integrated** with Calibre, Wallabag, Wikipedia...
- **extensible:** via plugins
- **full-featured:** multi-lingual user interface with a highly customizable reader view and many typeset options.
- **and much more:** look up words with StarDict dictionaries, add your own online OPDS catalogs and RSS feeds, share ebooks with other KOReader devices wirelessly...
